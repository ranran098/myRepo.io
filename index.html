Back in 1991, Delsan humbly began as a classic pera sa basura story. We saw an opportunity to turn empty cartridges into useful and economic products through toner recycling; helping save the environment and providing jobs to our fellow Filipinos. Ever since, we’ve stayed true to our mission to provide technological improvement that will satisfy and even exceed customers’ expectations. Thus, we have consistently evolved from an after-market toner remanufacturer to a business solutions provider, offering products and addressing current and future needs of our stakeholders.
